About Doctor Strange

Doctor Stephen Strange, once a brilliant but arrogant neurosurgeon, saw his life take a dramatic turn after a tragic accident robbed him of his surgical skills. In search of healing, he traveled the world and discovered the mystical arts. Under the guidance of the Ancient One, Strange learned to harness magic and protect the Earth from threats that science could not explain. From manipulating time with the Eye of Agamotto to traveling across dimensions, Doctor Strange became the Sorcerer Supreme — the ultimate guardian against dark forces. His story is one of transformation, sacrifice, and wisdom, reminding us that true strength lies not in what we hold, but in what we are willing to let go.
